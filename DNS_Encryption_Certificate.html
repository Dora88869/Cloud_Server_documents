Encryption certificate and DNS

Linking domain name to public IP address of virtual machine and then obtaining a TLS certifcate

this is the fourth step

1. Log in to GoDaddy

Go to GoDaddy and sign in to the account.

2. Access DNS Settings

  • Click **My Products** → Select your domain.
  • Click ** DNS** to open the DNS settings.

3. Add an A Record

  • Find the **A (Type) Record** section.
  • If an **A record** already exists, edit it. Otherwise, click **Add Record**.
  • Set the **Name** field to `@` (this represents your root domain).
  • Enter your **public IP address** in the **Value** field.
  • Set **TTL** (Time to Live) to the shortest available time for faster propagation.
  • Click **Save**.

5. Wait for DNS Propagation

Changes can take **a few minutes to 48 hours** to fully propagate.

Obtain a Let's Encrypt Digital Certificate

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Ensure Firewall Ports Are Open

For testing purposes, **TCP ports 22, 80, and 443** should be available through the firewall.

2. Test Your Website Over HTTP (Port 80)

Before obtaining a certificate, ensure your website is accessible over **HTTP (port 80)**.

3. Get Your SSL Certificate

  • Go to Certbot.
  • Select **Apache** on **Linux (snap)**.
  • Follow the provided instructions on the command prompt of the Ubantu Virtual Machine.

4. Verify HTTPS Setup

Once you've installed the certificate, visit website using **HTTPS (port 443)**.

Look for the lock icon in your browser’s address bar.
