She’s Got Caddy Issues - My Golf Journey Blog

Objective

This blog chronicles my many Golf challenges and issues. I will document my experiences, the new things I’ve learned, and the fun my friends and I have playing Golf. The blog aims to provide engaging stories that will resonate with fellow Golfers. It is not only a personal diary of my Golf adventures but also aims to be informative.

Content

  • Personal Golf experience – Tracking my progress, challenges, and best shots.
  • Golf course reviews – Highlights and low points of the courses I play at.
  • Training and practice – Exploring driving ranges and building strength for a better swing.
  • Equipment reviews – Assessing purchases by my friends and me to determine their value.
  • Snack review – Identifying the best and worst snacks to eat while golfing.
  • Bloopers – Fun moments, mistakes, and humorous experiences on the course.
  • Golf humor – Sharing Golf memes and jokes.

Multimedia

This blog will feature a photo gallery of my Golf adventures, from swings to wildlife encountered on the Golf course.

Conclusion

I hope this blog will showcase my love and passion for Golf, even if it can be the most frustrating game at times.

Golf is something I enjoy whenever I have time. My friends and I play **18 holes every month** as part of our **2025 New Year resolution**. We also play **9 holes after work** most weeks and head to the **driving range** whenever we’re free.
